"Clear Logo" Template for Lime Survey
--------------------------------------

Developed by James Neill (http://www.wilderdom.com).

Aims to provide a simple, user-friendly layout, with a logo in the top-left.

License: GPL v2.

To use your own logo, replace the logo.png file.

The example logo is 200 x 35 pixels, but there is plenty of space for a logo of your own size.
